1. Who We Are

Nora AI ("Company", "we", "us", "our") operates the Nora mobile application ("App") available on iOS and Android platforms. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal data responsibly.

Data Controller: Nora AI

Contact: privacy@nora-ai.org

By using the Nora App, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use our App.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Account information: Email address, name, and password when you register
  • Profile settings: AI companion name, preferred voice, language, and mode preferences
  • Conversation content: Text transcriptions of your voice conversations with Nora
  • Reminders and journal entries: Content you create within the App
  • Voice recordings: Audio captured during conversations (processed in real-time, not stored permanently — see Section 4)
  • Cloned voice data: Voice model created if you purchase the Voice Clone feature

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device information: Device type, operating system, app version, and device identifiers
  • Usage data: Features used, session duration, message counts, and interaction patterns
  • Performance data: Crash reports, error logs, and app performance metrics
  • Network information: IP address (used for security and fraud prevention only)

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Payment card information (handled exclusively by Apple/Google)
  • Precise geolocation data
  • Contacts, photos, or files on your device
  • Biometric data (other than voice for cloning, with explicit consent)
  • Data from other apps on your device

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • Service delivery: To provide, operate, and maintain the Nora App
  • Personalization: To personalize your AI companion experience based on your preferences and history
  • AI responses: To generate contextually relevant and emotionally intelligent responses
  • Notifications: To send reminders and check-ins you have requested
  • Security: To detect, prevent, and address fraud, abuse, and security incidents
  • Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and improve our services (using anonymized/aggregated data only)
  • Legal compliance: To comply with applicable laws and regulations
  • Customer support: To respond to your inquiries and resolve issues

✅ We do not use your data for advertising purposes. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with advertisers or marketing companies.

4. Voice Data & AI Processing

4.1 Voice Recordings

When you speak to Nora:

  • Your voice is captured locally on your device
  • The audio is transmitted securely to our speech-to-text service (ElevenLabs) for transcription
  • Audio recordings are deleted immediately after transcription — we do not store raw audio files
  • Only the text transcription is retained as part of your conversation history

4.2 AI Response Generation

  • Your conversation text is processed by our AI engine to generate responses
  • Conversations may include context from your previous interactions to provide memory features
  • AI processing occurs on secure third-party servers

4.3 Voice Cloning (Optional Feature)

⚠️ Important Notice: By using the Voice Clone feature, you provide explicit consent to create a voice model based on your recording. You confirm that: (a) the voice you record is your own; (b) you are at least 18 years old or have parental consent; (c) you will not misuse the cloned voice for fraud, impersonation, or deception.

  • Your voice sample (30–60 seconds) is sent to ElevenLabs to create a voice model
  • The voice model is stored on ElevenLabs servers and linked to your account
  • You can delete your cloned voice at any time in Settings → Voice Clone → Delete
  • Upon account deletion, your cloned voice model is permanently deleted within 30 days

6. Data Storage & Security

  • Location: Data is stored on Google Firebase servers located in the United States and European Union
  • Encryption in transit: All data transmitted uses TLS 1.3 encryption
  • Encryption at rest: All stored data is encrypted using AES-256
  • Access controls: Only authorized personnel with a legitimate business need can access user data
  • Security audits: We regularly review and update our security practices
  • Breach notification: In the event of a data breach affecting your rights, we will notify you within 72 hours as required by GDPR

7. Data Retention

  • Account data: Retained until you delete your account
  • Conversation history: Free plan — 3 days; Pro/Max plans — indefinitely until deleted
  • Voice recordings: Deleted immediately after transcription (within seconds)
  • Cloned voice model: Retained until you delete it or delete your account
  • Usage logs: Retained for 90 days for security purposes
  • After account deletion: All personal data is permanently deleted within 30 days, except where required by law

8. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Nora is available to users of all ages. Our Child Mode is specifically designed for children aged 6–12. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar international regulations.

  • Age requirement: Users under 13 require verifiable parental consent
  • Child Mode: When enabled, all content is filtered to be age-appropriate; certain features are disabled
  • Parental rights: Parents/guardians can review, modify, or delete their child's data by contacting us at privacy@nora-ai.org
  • No behavioral advertising: We never use children's data for advertising purposes
  • Voice data: We do not create voice clones for users under 18
  • If we discover we have collected data from a child under 13 without verifiable parental consent, we will delete it immediately

9. EU/EEA Users (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Union or European Economic Area, the following applies:

9.1 Legal Basis for Processing

  • Contract performance: Processing necessary to provide the Nora service you have requested
  • Legitimate interests: Security, fraud prevention, and service improvement
  • Consent: For voice cloning and optional features
  • Legal obligation: When required by applicable law

9.2 Your GDPR Rights

  • Right of access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Right to rectification: Correct inaccurate personal data
  • Right to erasure: Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restriction: Request restriction of processing
  • Right to portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Right to object: Object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Right to withdraw consent: Withdraw consent at any time without affecting prior processing

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@nora-ai.org. We will respond within 30 days. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

10. California Users (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) provides you with additional rights:

  • Right to delete: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Right to opt-out: We do not sell personal information — no opt-out needed
  • Right to non-discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising your CCPA rights

To submit a CCPA request, contact us at privacy@nora-ai.org

11. Your Rights (All Users)
